
## Remember trig pairs

###  Sinx and Cosx
$$ \int sinx = -cosx + c $$

$$ \int cosx = sinx + c $$

$$ sin^{2}x + cos^{2}x = 1$$
### Secx and Tanx

$$ tan^{2}x + 1 = sec^{2}x $$
$$ \frac {d\:tanx} {dx} = sec^{2}x$$

### Cosecx and Cotx

$$ cot^{2}x + 1 = cosec^{2}x $$
$$\int cosec^{2}x = -cotx$$
$$\frac {d\:cosecx} {dx} = cosex\:cotx$$
