### Idea
#### Theorem 3.4.3
If f is continuous on a closed interval $[a, b]$ then $f$ attains its minimum and maximum on $[a,b]$. That is, there exist points c and d in $[a,b]$ such that
$$
f(c) \leq f(x) \leq f(d)
$$
for all x in $[a,b].$
